About Our Academy

Established with a vision to nurture cricket talent in the Sangli region

Developing Champions Since 2010

Millennium Cricket Academy in Sangli is a premier cricket training institution dedicated to identifying and nurturing young talent. With over a decade of experience, we have produced numerous district and state-level players.

Our academy follows a comprehensive training methodology that focuses on technical skills, physical fitness, mental toughness, and match awareness. We believe in holistic development of each cricketer.

Located in the heart of Sangli, our state-of-the-art facilities provide the perfect environment for cricketers to hone their skills and reach their full potential.
